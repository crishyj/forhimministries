  • Erbil, Iraq
    Erbil is the place of one of the oldest forts in the
    world. Alexander the Great was said to stop here
    as he continued his quest. The Curdish people are
    very strong and known for thier skills in battle. Interestingly, the Christians and Muslims live in harmony side by side. Yet Jesus is calling to their hearts and saving souls.
  • Entebe, Uganda
    The people are very receptive to Jesus Christ.
    Many are poor and need assistance. I had the
    privilage of praying for a woman who had aids.
    The power of God fell so that she fell to the ground under God's hand. Pastor Mutebi is the hardest
    working Man of God I have ever seen. I met with
    him on several occasions and helped him lay brick.
  • Le Ceiba, Honduras
    The Lord led in such a unique way here on
    multiple occasions. I was led to one of the case
    studies in my next book.

    While visiting the local town, we ran across a
    witch that started laughing at us. We began to
    speak Jesus' name and the witch stopped
    laughing and left the park very quickly.

    To God be the Glory!

Above are some places this ministry has been able to reach!
